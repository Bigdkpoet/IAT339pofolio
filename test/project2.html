The black humor

In Simon Fraser University New Media Images course(IAT 202), I and my team present a black humor micro movie, Robbery, which is inspired by Quentin.I participated in part of the scriptwriting, shooting, lighting editing and sound-mixing in the whole production process.

Working projct-progress 1: Pre-effect

Element Design

UI content,visual effect,Text

The first step is writing scripts and constructed a whole visual guideline for the movie. The very first problem we met is the division of the story. Base on the research on a related movie like Hongkong movie in 1990 and discussions we made, The final vision of our scripts comes out with a very dramatic and darkness style. When the progress moved to cast, we met the trouble which should not happen in teamwork, which is lacking communication. Through argument with the local actor, the problem was finally solved.

Before we start shooting and editing, we made a shot List to determine the sequence of our film. Meanwhile, base on the casting and related environment, the shot list changed many times. In the pre-production, lighting and sound recording were also important parts. It directly affects the final quality of filming production. Therefore, as the main charge of the lighting and sound recording, I need to deeply know the combination of post-production and visual language.

Working project 2-progress 2: filming & post production

Due to the limitation of equipment from the library, I met many problems, such as noise(low quality of the image because of lacking light), bad sound picking, and unpredictable shake from the stabilizer. This irreversible mistake always reminds me avoiding it in my future work and project. After all those preproduction work, all those footage were hand into my teammate for editing base on the script that we determined. With the ability of good communication and the knowledge and experience I learn from the School, I solved all those problems and gain a good relationship with a teammate. But most importantly, The feedback from the school film festival is good I learn more experience and knowledge than I expect.
